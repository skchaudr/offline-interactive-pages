# Brief: Obsidian + Neovim Power Fluency Lab

**Goal**: Accelerate an Obsidian power user (strong mental models around linking, search, daily notes, structured PKM) who is an emerging intermediate Neovim user toward power-user fluency and lightning-fast editing speed by leveraging familiar concepts as a bridge into modal editing, motions, text objects, and obsidian.nvim workflows.

**Why this matters**: Your Obsidian intuition is already excellent for information navigation and synthesis. Neovim adds surgical precision editing, repeatability, and composability. Combining them removes the "context switch tax" and turns note-taking/editing into a superpower.

**Audience**: Obsidian heavy users comfortable with plugins, search, linking, and daily notes who know basic Neovim (modal editing, some motions, perhaps LazyVim or similar setup) but want to internalize advanced fluency fast.

**Archetype**: Learning artifact

**Key Sections**:
- Mental model bridge: Obsidian concepts mapped to Neovim equivalents + power combos
- Interactive drills focused on markdown/PKM editing tasks
- obsidian.nvim specific workflows (quick switch, search, backlinks, templates, daily notes)
- Fluency accelerators (which-key discovery, motion plugins, custom leader maps that feel native)
- Progress tracker + spaced practice prompts using localStorage
- Actionable next steps for your actual vault

**Constraints**: Single-file `index.html`, fully offline, follows RULES.md and performance budget, uses patterns from shared/ and existing pages (e.g. 006), educational + immediately actionable.

**Persistence**: Track mastered mappings/drills in localStorage.

**Sources**: Self-contained synthesis of common obsidian.nvim + Neovim best practices for PKM users. Builds directly on the Neovim mental models from pages/006-neovim-lazyvim-lab.
