1. Anatomy of a URL

A Uniform Resource Locator (URL) is the address of a resource on the web. Click the parts below to understand what they do.

https:// api.example.com :443 /v1/users/123 ?include_profile=true
Hover or click a URL part above

A complete URL provides the protocol to use, the server's location, and the specific resource being requested.
