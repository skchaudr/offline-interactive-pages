obsidian.nvim Power Workflows

Daily Notes & Templates

<leader>on New daily note
<leader>ot Templates

One keystroke for perfectly templated daily notes.

Search & Navigation

<leader>os Quick Switch
<leader>of Find files / notes
<leader>ob Backlinks

Telescope + obsidian.nvim = Obsidian search with full Neovim power.

Recommended Setup Tips (obsidian.nvim + LazyVim)

  • Use which-key to discover all <leader>o* mappings
  • Enable obsidian completion source for [[ linking
  • Pair with flash.nvim or leap.nvim for instant jumps in long notes
  • Map a leader key for “follow link under cursor” that feels like Obsidian clicking

Discovery

Never hunt again:

Motion & Editing Speed

  • Leap / Flash: Jump to any visible char in 2–3 keys
  • Surround: ysiw`, cs"', ds"
  • Text objects: ciw, ca(, vi" in markdown
  • Repeat: . for repetitive PKM edits
